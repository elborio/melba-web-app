Stamppot boerenkool

The traditional dutch winter meal. Easy to cook and prepare, but it takes some time to prepare the potatos.

  • € 2.45 p.p.
  • Beginner
  • 30m

Ingredients

  • 2 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 spoon honey
  • 400 gram pork

Instruction

  1. Preheat oven to 200Â°.
  2. In a saucepan, bring broth and salt to a boil.
  3. Add in rice; return to a boil.
  4. Transfer to a 2 1/2 quart casserole dish that has been lightly coated with cooking spray.
  5. Add in spice, honey, butter, peaches, and nuts; mix well.
  6. Cover and bake about 45 minutes or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed.
